    EXECUTIVE COUNCIL COMMITTEE

    Shri.Ramraoji Lohat
    Institute President
    Prof.Nitin Lohat
    Institute Secretary
    Since it's established in 2002 the Matoshri Jijau gramvikas Mandal has strived hard and succeeded to achieve two objectives. The first is the spread of education to the door step of the poor and needful students; the second one is imparting high quality of education.
    We strongly believe that every student is a unique person with his/her own set of abilities, needs and aspirations. We are committed to bring out that uniqueness. Besides academic excellence, we committed to inculcate in all its students strong ethical values of integrity, politeness, kindness and respect for elders.
    I firmly believe that education should foster confidence, disciplines, clarity in thoughts and decision making ability to set and achieve goals and above all, social responsibility as a lifelong process.
